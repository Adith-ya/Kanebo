Kanebo Short Story
Kanebo is and interior platform that provides a variety of furnitures and interiors to decorate your room. Kanebo is already working with the artist from all over the world to help them sell their art to customers through this platform. This platform is used by up to 100 million people every day and has been certified as the best platform in the world. Kanebo has cooperated with national and international security which has been protected by the state and has been awarded as the best platform with the strongest security in the world.
Fun Fact About Kanebo
Kanebo is a web platform created by four students from indonesia. This platform was designed from four vocational students as a school assignment project. The results of their work are appreciated by foreign companies and then they are financed by the state to develope this project. several years later after this project was succesfull. Kanebo has become a web platform with the most users in the world with a profit rate reaching billions of dollars a day.
